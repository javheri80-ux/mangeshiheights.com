Why Families Are Moving to Balkum Pada

Balkum Pada is perfectly positioned to connect you to major roadways while remaining slightly away from the heaviest traffic corridors. Living at Mangeshi Heights means you can get around the Mumbai Metropolitan Region (MMR) with ease:

  • Road Connectivity: Just 1.5 KM (5 minutes) from the Eastern Express Highway (EEH) and direct access to Ghodbunder Road.
  • Rail & Metro Access: Thane Railway Station is only 6 KM away, and the proposed Balkum Metro Station is within walking distance.
  • Workplaces: Close proximity to Kolshet Industrial Area, ThaneOne Corporate Business IT Park, and Orion Business Park.
