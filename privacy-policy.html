Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Your privacy is incredibly important to us at Mangeshi Heights (mangeshiheights.com). This policy outlines how we gather, manage, and protect your information when you visit or interact with our site. We want to be entirely clear about how your data is handled so you can browse and inquire with confidence.

2. Information We Collect

We only request details that are necessary to help you in your home-buying journey. This includes:

  • Information you send us: When you fill out our contact forms, request a call back, or enter captcha codes, we collect details like your name, phone number, and email address.
  • Technical visit data: Anonymous info such as IP address, browser type, device details, and site usage statistics to help us maintain a fast and reliable website.
  • Cookies: We use simple cookies to remember your preferences and make your experience smoother. You can easily adjust your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer.

3. How We Use Your Information

The details we collect are used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries with accurate pricing, floor layouts, and scheduling site visits.
  • Keep our website running smoothly and optimize the layout for mobile and desktop users.
  • Send updates, project newsletters, or construction progress reports (only if you have agreed to receive them).
  • Maintain compliance with legal standards and regulatory guidelines.

4. Data Security

We take data security seriously. We use secure servers and industry-standard protocols to prevent unauthorized access or sharing of your personal details. While no system on the internet is completely bulletproof, we do our utmost to keep your records safe.

5. Sharing Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We only share details with the official developers or trusted affiliates directly responsible for facilitating your inquiry. They are bound by strict confidentiality terms.

6. Third-Party Web Links

Our site contains helpful links to external resources (such as RERA portals). Once you leave our domain, please note that we do not control the privacy practices of those external websites.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, want us to update your details, or wish to request data removal, please contact us at info@mangeshiheights.com or call +91 8237498373.
